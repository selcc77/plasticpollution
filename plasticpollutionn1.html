Steps to Get Involved

  1. Organize student-led cleanup drives along the Caspian coastline and urban areas.
  2. Partner with local recycling centers like AzEkol LLC to process the collected plastic.
  3. Collaborate with grocery stores to sell and promote the recycled grocery bags.
  4. Raise awareness about the importance of sustainable practices through campaigns and workshops.
